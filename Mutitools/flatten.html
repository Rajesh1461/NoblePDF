What Happens When You Flatten a PDF?
  • Annotations: Comments, highlights, and drawings become part of the page content
  • Form Fields: Fillable forms are converted to static text and images
  • Layers: Multiple layers are merged into a single layer
  • Result: PDF becomes non-editable but maintains visual appearance
